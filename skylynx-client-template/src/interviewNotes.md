## Overview

The **Geospatial Pod (geoPod)** model is tailored specifically for GIS operations, automation, and data integrity workstreams.  
Each geoPod supports one or more projects, clients, or utility zones.  
geoPods are managed under the **Director of Geospatial Systems & Innovation**, and scaled to optimize throughput, training, and reuse of geospatial assets.

## 🏥 Healthcare Software Engineering – Acrindo Software & eMobile Health

### Acrindo Software: Data Model Compliance & Secure System Integration

At Acrindo Software, I led efforts to ensure that all core data models and application logic adhered to **HIPAA compliance standards** across their healthcare platforms. This included:

- Implementing secure user profile management for Rx systems and accounting modules  
- Designing access controls, audit logging, and PHI (Protected Health Information) handling in alignment with federal compliance rules  
- Supporting integration between **prescription management**, **billing**, and **role-based access systems** to ensure proper separation of duties and data visibility  

To meet HIPAA requirements, the following high-level practices were implemented:

- **Classified PHI across Profile & Rx records** to enforce data protection scope  
- **Role-based access controls** to limit data visibility by job function  
- **Minimum necessary access** principles across billing, Rx, and profile modules  
- **Audit logging and change tracking** for all PHI access and updates  
- **Encryption in transit and at rest** to secure data storage and transmission  
- **De-identification** protocols for non-clinical reporting and analytics  
- **Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)** with vendors handling PHI  
- **Regular risk assessments** to uncover vulnerabilities and misconfigurations  
- **User authentication and identity verification** for profile and prescription systems  
- **Staff training and documented privacy policies** to enforce compliance culture  
- **PHI Retention and Archival** policies implemented based on federal/state regulations (e.g., 6-year minimum per HIPAA, longer for certain Rx records)  
- **Telehealth Video Session Retention** rules reviewed; where applicable, recordings were either:  
  - Not stored at all (live-stream only), or  
  - Retained in **HIPAA-compliant storage** with access control, audit logging, and deletion policies (if part of medical record)  

This foundational work ensured patient safety, legal compliance, and operational efficiency for clinics using Acrindo’s software.
