#Schedule optimization and dispatch

Management Schedule optimization & dispatch is a solution that helps service organizations to plan, execute, and optimize their service delivery.



It uses artificial intelligence, real-time data, and self-learning algorithms to assign tasks to the best available technicians, predict task duration and travel time, and adjust schedules dynamically. It also provides tools for managing parts, online appointments, exceptions, mapping, and collaboration.



Service management software aims to reduce costs by increasing first time fix rates and SLA hit rates for your service business, improve customer satisfaction, and lower environmental impact.
